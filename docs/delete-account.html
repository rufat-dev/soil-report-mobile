How to request account deletion

You can request account deletion directly in the app:

  1. Open Soil Report.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm by tapping Delete Account in the confirmation dialog.

What happens when you delete your account

  • Your user account is marked as deleted and access to your account is removed.
  • You are signed out of the app.
  • Your account is no longer available for normal app use.

Data deleted and data retained

  • Deleted/disabled for use: account profile and account accessibility in the app.
  • May be retained for limited time: security, fraud-prevention, and legal compliance records, if required.
  • Retention basis: retained only where required by law, security, or legitimate operational necessity.
If you cannot access the app and still want your account deleted, contact support by email below.
